Outreach

Community outreach is very important to the Caltech Robotics Team! From running a booth at a STEM night with local Girl Scouts, to giving tours of our lab to groups of aspiring high school students, we're always looking for more ways to get students interested in robotics! If you would like our team to help out with a STEM event, or would like to organize a tour, please feel free to reach out to us from the contact form on our website.
